Quick Flashlight

Android app that turn on the flash light and set screen brightness to max on start, as fast as possible.

Installation

From Google Play

Usage

Just tap the icon of the app :) On start, the application turns on the camera flashlight if your device have one, displays a white screen and set the screen brightness to maximum.
